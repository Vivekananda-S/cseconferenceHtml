Convener


Dr. Anupriya Elumalai

Professor & Head of Department – Computer Science and Engineering

Dr. E. Anupriya is a self motivated and task oriented professional with 25 years of experience in Academia and Industry. She completed her B.E.(CSE), M.Tech.(CSE) and Ph.D.(CSE) from VIT University, Vellore, Tamil Nadu.
She has 23 publications towards her credit in peer reviewed journals and conferences. Her current research interest includes solving problems using Machine Learning and Deep Learning techniques.
She has diversified working track in different countries like Malaysia, Lebanon and Oman in different capacities. She has led different teams of faculty and staff under her leadership as Head of Department and Research Chairmanship for 5 years. She has hosted 15 workshops and FDPs in the last two years. She consistently strives for the holistic development of student’s community.
She has received ATAL FDP grant of 0.93 Lakhs for the academic year 2020-2021 for hosting FDP on “Object detection and recognition using Deep Learning Techniques.”
